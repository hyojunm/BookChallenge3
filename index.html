** EXACT DATES ARE NOT PROVIDED BY THE BOOK **

  • 19 years ago - Est. La Cocina

    19 years ago when the Mr. Merritt, a big customer of Veronica Zamora, passed away, he left some money for her. With it Veronica Zamora established the restaurant of La Cocina de la Isla.

  • Every Sunday - Sunday Family Dinner

    Every Sunday evenings, all of the Zamoras gather up in the restaurant to have a family dinner with every member in the family. This included Arturo Zamora's (main character) grandparents, cousins, aunts, uncles, and even friends. In this time the family prayed, ate, and discussed about their business.

  • Beginning of Summer - Arturo Zamora is Hired

    In the beginning of summer break, Arturo Zamora (7th grade) was hired as the dishwasher of La Cocina to earn money. He is going to be under the control of Martin Zamora in the kitchen.

  • Middle of Summer - Puts on a bid for the Lot

    This summer, La Cocina put a bid for the lot next to the restaurant to expand the dining space. The city is going to sell the property to the winner, and so far nobody except for this restaurant applied.

  • Middle of Summer - Wilfrido Pipo puts in a Bid

    Shortly after his arrival to Canal Grove, Florida, Wilfrido Pipo, CEO of Pipo Land Holdings, LLC, placed a bid for the lot next to La Cocina. He wanted to build a luxury apartment called Pipo Place with various entertainment and grocery.

  • End of Summer - Veronica Zamora's Memorial Dinner

    After the death of Veronica Zamora, Cari Zamora (Veronica's daughter) decided to host a memorial dinner, remebering what Veronica Zamora did to this community, and how it impacted the city.

  • Middle of Summer - W. Pipo Arrive @ Canal Grove

    With experience of building Pipo Place in more than 5 cities, Wilfrido Pipo this time visited Canal Grove to establish Pipo Place again. He planned to put in a bid for the lot next to La Cocina and build the building there.

  • Middle of Summer - Hosts Parties & Catered Lunches

    To advertise for his Pipo Place, Wilfrido Pipo has hosted a lot of parties for people and special catered lunch for nearby businesses. In those parties and lunches he presented his plan of how Pipo Place will look, have, and how it will benefit them.

  • End of Summer - Hosts a Big Party

    Only a few days before the vote, W. Pipo again hosted a party for everyone in the neighborhood, giving out free food, gifts, and making speeches to persuade people to support Pipo Place. In this event La Cocina is worried and gives out flyers that persuades people to support them.

  • End of Summer - W. Pipo Leaves Canal Grove

    After hearing about the results of the vote held a few days ago, Wilfrido Pipo, CEO of Pipo Land Holdings, LLC, decided to leave Canal Grove and find another place to establish yet another Pipo Place. Will he succeed this time?
